About

Dr. Shili Mohamed

Dr. Shili Mohamed earned his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering with distinction. His research interests span the Internet of Things (IoT), e-commerce, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), and mobile agents.

Born in Kairouan, Tunisia (1989)

Dr. Mohamed completed his bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2010. He earned his Master’s degree in Intelligent Pervasive Systems from the University of Sousse in 2016. He obtained his Ph.D. in 2024 from the National School of Engineering of Carthage, University of Carthage, Tunisia.

Reviewer & Researcher

Dr. Mohamed has reviewed over 50 articles for the IEEE Access journal and the Computers and Electrical Engineering journal. His research interests include Distributed Generation, Wireless Sensor Networks, GIS, Recommender Systems, and Artificial Intelligence (AI).
